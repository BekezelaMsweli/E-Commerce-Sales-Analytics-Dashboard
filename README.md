# E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data to uncover insights into revenue performance, customer purchasing behavior, product profitability, sales trends, and customer retention. An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to support data-driven decision-making and identify opportunities for business growth.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify top revenue-generating products
- Analyze customer spending behavior
- Track monthly sales trends
- Evaluate sales performance by country
- Measure customer retention and repeat purchases

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- SQL
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Excel / CSV

## 📊 Key Findings
- **DOTCOM POSTAGE** generated the highest revenue.
- **CustomerID 14646** was the highest-spending customer.
- **November** recorded the highest sales performance, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- The **United Kingdom** contributed the largest share of total revenue.
- Most customers made only one purchase, highlighting low customer retention.

## 💡 Recommendations
- Implement customer loyalty and rewards programs.
- Increase marketing efforts before peak sales periods.
- Use personalized promotions to encourage repeat purchases.
- Focus on retaining high-value customers through targeted engagement strategies.

## 📈 Business Impact
The dashboard provides a clear view of sales performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions, improve customer retention, and optimize sales strategies.
